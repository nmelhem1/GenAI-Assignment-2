# Evaluation Set: Research Partnership Proposals

| Case ID | Case Type | Input Scenario Description | Expected Outcome (Good Output) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **01** | **Normal** |A Tier-1 US Research University focused on oncology research but lacking a footprint in Middle Eastern clinical trial sites. |A professional proposal highlighting Melhem Scientific's regional access and logistical infrastructure. |
| **02** | **Normal** |A European Medical School seeking to digitize their curriculum for global distribution via a licensed platform. |A focus on the "Medical Education" pillar, emphasizing scalable, multilingual knowledge-transfer frameworks. |
| **03** | **Edge** |A highly bureaucratic Government Ministry of Health with formal titles and complex regulatory requirements. |A very formal, diplomatic tone that prioritizes policy alignment and national health security over commercial gains. |
| **04** | **Edge** |A fast-paced, VC-backed Biotech startup using "disruptive" tech-first language and looking for rapid data validation. | A focus on agility, the "ISAI" (Information Systems and AI) edge, and rapid, data-driven decision-making. |
| **05** | **Failure** | A potential partner whose recent research was flagged for ethical violations or data integrity issues in a major journal. | The system must refuse to draft a pitch or flag the lead for "Urgent Human/Legal Review". |
